Policies

Inclusive, respectful, and student-centered.

STI policies protect our community, clarify expectations, and ensure students can focus on their music education.

Non-discrimination policy

Inclusive learning community

Seattle Tabla Institute is committed to providing an inclusive and welcoming environment for all members of our community. We do not discriminate in access to educational programs or employment opportunities based on race, color, gender, national origin, age, religion, creed, disability, veteran status, or sexual orientation.

This policy ensures that equitable and consistent standards of conduct and performance are applied in accordance with federal and state laws and regulations.

Class policies and guidelines

Clear expectations

Enrollment and tuition

  • New students start with an intro pack before ongoing classes.
  • Classes are weekly year-round with seasonal breaks.
  • Tuition is paid in advance for each class series or semester.

Attendance and make-up classes

  • Students can request a recorded class when absent.
  • Make-up classes are offered based on availability.
  • Make-up sessions should be scheduled within 60 days.

Student responsibilities

  • Regular practice outside of class is expected.
  • Parents escort youth students to and from class.
  • Respectful conduct is required in all settings.

Instructor absences

  • Substitutes may be provided when available.
  • Rescheduled classes will be announced in advance.
  • STI may cancel or adjust classes for emergencies.

Contact STI if you have questions about class policies or scheduling.
