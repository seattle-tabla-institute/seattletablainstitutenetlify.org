Event goals

Education first

Community learning

Each event is designed to educate audiences about rhythm, improvisation, and the cultural history of tabla.

  • Artist-led lecture demonstrations
  • Family-friendly performances
  • Opportunities to ask questions

Collaborative partnerships

We work with regional artists, venues, and educators to broaden access to South Asian classical music.

  • Guest artist residencies
  • Community recitals in public spaces
  • Local venue partnerships
