About STI

Rooted in tradition, open to the community.

Seattle Tabla Institute (STI) is an incorporated 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded in 2009 to encourage and support the art of tabla drumming and Hindustani music in Washington.

EIN 27-2849217

Mission

Our mission is to teach tabla through high-quality educational programs and to present public performances that connect the community with South Asian classical music.

We aim to make rigorous training accessible while preserving the depth and lineage of Hindustani music.

History

STI was founded by a group of students, teachers, and community musicians who wanted a dedicated learning home for tabla in the Seattle area. Since 2010 we have delivered thousands of hours of music education through classes, workshops, and concerts.
