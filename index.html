Tabla education in Seattle

Learn tabla. Build community. Celebrate Hindustani music.

Seattle Tabla Institute (STI) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to teaching tabla to youth and adults and hosting public events that connect the community with South Asian classical music.

Programs for youth and adults

In-person and online

Youth programs (ages 5-18)

Small group classes focused on foundational technique, rhythm, confidence, and performance readiness.

  • Beginner to advanced tracks
  • Hybrid classes for busy families
  • Annual youth recital and exams
View youth classes

Adult programs (ages 19+)

Learn accompaniment, performance, and advanced repertoire with structured curriculum and personalized guidance.

  • Intro packs for new students
  • Weekly skill-building cohorts
  • Performance opportunities
